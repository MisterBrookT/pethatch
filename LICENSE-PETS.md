Pet assets are separate creative works. Each pet declares its asset license in `pet.json`.

Current pets:

| Pet | Asset License | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `xiaochai` | CC-BY-NC-4.0 | Generated original Shiba Inu pet pack for PetHatch. |

If you own rights to an asset and believe it should not be distributed here, open an issue with the pet id and proof of ownership. We will remove or disable the pet while reviewing the request.
